A New Beginning; My Redeemeers Journey
The path to Cybersecurity; My professional journey officially began in
2024, marked by the rigorous preparation for the JAMB examinations,
where Mathematics and Physics were my core focus areas. This
period was crucial, as it forged a resilient, logic-driven approach to
complex problems-a necessary skill in Cybersecurity.

Following this preparatory phase, I successfully secured admission to
study Information Technology at Redeemers University. My academic
focus remains deeply commited to mastering the fundamentals of IT,
which directly supports my cybersecurity aspirations.

As a dedicated 200L student, I am commited to mastering the basic
foundational IT concepts especially Networking and Operating Systems
because you cannot secure what you do not fully understand. However I,
understand that for cybersecurity, the "how" is more important than the
"what". For example, while I know the OSI model, my current, urgent
focus is on deeply understanding the behaviour within Layers 3 and 4.

This is where my "Redeemers Journey" truly manifests: I am not content
with theoretical knowledge alone; towards fluency in the tools that expose
that knowledge in action.
